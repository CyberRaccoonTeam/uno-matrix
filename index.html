📖 HOW TO PLAY
🎯
Goal: Be the first to get rid of all your cards. Match the top discard card by color or number.
🃏
Number Cards (0–9): Play if color OR number matches. Worth their face value in scoring.
⊘
Skip: Next player loses their turn. Worth 20 points.
↺
Reverse: Flip the direction of play. In 2-player, acts as a Skip. Worth 20 points.
+2
Draw Two: Next player draws 2 cards and loses their turn. Stackable! Worth 20 points.
★
Wild: Play on any card. Choose the new color. Worth 50 points.
+4
Wild Draw Four: Choose color AND next player draws 4. Stackable on another +4. Worth 50 points.
🚨
UNO! When you have 1 card left, hit the UNO button before playing. Miss it and draw 2 penalty cards!
🏆
Scoring: When someone wins, they score points based on cards remaining in other players' hands.
