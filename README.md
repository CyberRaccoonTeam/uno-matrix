## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Match cards** by color or number to the discard pile
2. **First to empty** their hand wins the round
3. **Call UNO** when you have one card left (automatic in this version)
4. **Special Cards:**
   - **Skip** - Next player loses their turn
   - **Reverse** - Changes direction of play
   - **+2** - Next player draws 2 cards and loses turn
   - **Wild** - Choose the next color
   - **Wild +4** - Choose color AND next player draws 4
